Dogs

DOg Information

Dogs are popular pets because people enjoy a dogs's company, but keeping a dog is hardwork. Dogs need lots of exercise and have to be take out for a walk at least twice a day. They also need to be fed at regular mealtimes and have water available at all times. Dog should be brushed and cleaned to keep them healthy. Some of the basic things needed include a collar, a leash, a bed, food and water dishes, dog food, toys to play with, and brushes for grooming.
